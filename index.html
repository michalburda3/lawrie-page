about us

The vehicles used by L and R Logistics are of the highest standard – their Blue Motion technology support London’s low emission zone. All vehicles are serviced regularly and equipped to carry any item safely and securely. We are fully insured for goods in transit and public liability. Terms and conditions, cancellation and payment policies.

We are based in Leatherhead and are happy to cover all the surrounding areas (I.e. Cobham, Guildford, Dorking, Epsom, Esher, anywhere Surrey, London and the UK)

Who we are?



Lawrence Erasmus is the founder of the company. He started in the transport industry in 2009 driving medical samples and pay cheques throughout the UK and working for a few courier and removals companies on an "ad-hoc" basis. Through hard work and perseverance he was able to secure a few private request clients and the company was born.

Lawrence is an extremely hard worker, he is up for any challenge, both physical and technical, and is always ready to dispense help and advice along the way. His cheerfulness and charm definitely help toward making each job quick, easy and stress free for the clients. Everyone employed and sourced to work with us, share the exact same positive attributes.

Please note: we do not offer the option of self loading the vehicles, we prefer to do it for you.We do offer a packing, wrapping and boxing service as an optional extra.
